Stanley Lau

Artist Bio

Formally trained in graphic design and advertising, Stanley’s art is imbued with a strong sense of aesthetics and visual fluidity. It is a perfect blend of eastern and western art styles. Better known by his handle Artgerm, Stanley’s art continues to infect and inspire new generations of artists and his ever-growing fan base around the world. To this date, Stanley’s online art gallery on deviantART has been viewed for more than 50 million times and has 500,000 active followers around the world.
